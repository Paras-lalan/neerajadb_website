ABOUT NEERAJA DISTILLERIES AND BREWERIES

About Neeraja

Tradition since 2019.

Neeraja is a Distilleries & Breweries company set up in 2019. We started manufacturing high quality all kind of alcoholic spirits, including India Made Foreign Liquor as well as Country Liquor, Beverage, Wines and others similar products, such as brandy, whisky, rum, gin, beer and all other alcoholic products whether made of natural or synthetic materials and increased our workforce from 2 core founders within a span of a few months. We've taken a giant leap over our competitors in the market by providing top quality service, affordable prices, meeting deadlines and applying all the latest process wherever required We are a constantly evolving company: we keep using the latest available technologies and therefore always updated with the current trends in the market. Our clients all across the world like to work with us because of the sole purpose that we always deliver the project on time along with a top quality.
